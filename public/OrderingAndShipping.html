Ordering and Shipping

Time and price

The expected delivery date is indicated in the purchase procedure and in the order confirmation email.

In case of non-delivery due to the absence of the recipient, the courier will leave a notice of passage with the shipment details.
The courier may make a second delivery attempt. If the second attempt is not successful, the package will be deposited in storage at the courier's branch and will be available for collection within 5 calendar days, starting from the day following the date of the notice of passage.

Standard 4/5 days:
  • Free for orders over 19€
  • 2,70€ for orders above 19€
Express 2/3 days: o nly for book in stock
  • 4,00€ for orders over 50€
  • 2,50€ for orders above 50€

The package will remain at the collection point for a maximum of 30 days.
After these days the package will be returned to our warehouse and another order will have to be made.
