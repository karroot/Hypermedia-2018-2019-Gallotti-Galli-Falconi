An eBook is a book in digital format. It is therefore a file that can be read using appropriate devices such as eReaders, computers or applications on smartphones and tablets.

The book in digital format usually presents the same contents as the book in paper format, what differentiates it is the mode of use. The eBooks are produced in various formats, to distinguish them it is sufficient to read the description of the product supplied in the online form. It is important to pay attention to the format of the eBook: in fact, each format corresponds to specific characteristics in terms of display and devices that allow reading.

ePup is format is the world standard for eBooks. It has the advantage of having a dynamic pagination. This means that the format of the pages is variable and adapts to the device with which you are reading the eBook. You can customize the reading, for example, by changing the font size.

I bought an eBook and when I open it on Adobe Digital Editions I get the error E_LIC_ALREADY_FULFILLED_BY_ANOTHER_USER. What should I do?
This error appears if the eBook has not been opened correctly or if the Adobe ID you are trying to authorize is incorrect: contact us, providing the order number, your e-mail with which you made the order 'purchase and the eBook that gives you this error, you will be given a new license.

I bought an eBook and when I open it on Adobe Digital Editions I get the error E_ACT_NOT_READY / E_AUTH_NOT_READY. What should I do?
If this error appears, it means there is a communication problem with the Adobe license server. This error is usually solved by authorizing and re-authoring Adobe Digital Editions.
To deauthorize Adobe Digital Editions (ie disconnect the Adobe ID from the program), press at the same time CTRL SHIFT and the letter D: a window will appear where it will ask for confirmation of the cancellation of the authorization information. Confirm the deauthorization and close Adobe Digital Editions. Now reopen the program and press again at the same time CTRL SHIFT and the letter D: this time a window will appear asking for authorization information. At this point, enter your Adobe ID as well, confirm the operation, and open the eBook again, which presented the error to read it correctly.
If the problem is not resolved with this procedure, contact us and we will help you with the resolution.

I'm using the Android / iOS app and the error "Connection Error" appears. What should I do?
First you need to check the connection to the internet. If the connection is active, check from the device settings that the date and time set are correct. If even this does not resolve the problem, uninstall the application and reinstall it via your device's store.
